Garden Maintainance

This course focuses on the practical skills needed to maintain gardens and outdoor spaces. Learn about lawn care, weeding, pruning, pest control, and seasonal garden upkeep. The training emphasizes both efficiency and sustainability, preparing you to maintain gardens that thrive year-round.

What You'll Learn:

  • Lawn care and mowing techniques
  • Pruning and trimming plants
  • Weeding and soil management
  • Pest and disease control
  • Seasonal garden maintenance

R750

One time payment

Ready to keep gardens flourishing all year round? This course is perfect for anyone wanting to master practical garden maintenance skills. From proper pruning techniques to managing common pests organically, you'll build real expertise through hands-on training with professional tools and methods. Whether you're caring for your own garden or starting a maintenance business, this course gives you the practical skills and knowledge to keep any garden healthy and beautiful.

Jabu Molefe

An experienced horticulturist and garden maintenance specialist with over 22 years of hands-on experience caring for South African gardens, Jabu has an intuitive understanding of what plants need to thrive. Starting his career as a gardener and progressing through formal horticultural training, he now runs a successful garden maintenance company and shares his extensive knowledge through training programs. His practical, no-nonsense teaching approach and deep understanding of South African plants, climate, and common challenges make garden maintenance accessible to everyone. His emphasis on sustainable practices and working with nature rather than against it has helped countless homeowners and aspiring professionals develop green thumbs.
