Sewing

This course equips learners with sewing, tailoring, and pattern-making techniques. You will explore hand-stitching, fabric cutting, machine sewing, alterations, and advanced tailoring skills. By the end, you will be able to create and repair garments confidently, unlocking opportunities in fashion and tailoring businesses

What You'll Learn:

  • Basic and advanced stitching techniques
  • Pattern making and fabric cutting
  • Garment fitting and alterations
  • Finishing techniques for professional results
  • Clothing repair and upcycling

R1500

One time payment

Ready to thread your way to creativity? This course is perfect for anyone wanting to develop practical sewing skills or explore their creative side. From mending torn clothing to creating your own designs, you'll build real confidence with needle and thread in a supportive, encouraging environment. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refine existing skills, this course gives you the techniques and creative confidence to turn fabric into something beautiful.

Zanele Dlamini

A passionate fashion designer and sewing instructor with over 18 years of experience, Zanele combines traditional African textile techniques with modern design principles. Trained at the Durban University of Technology's Fashion Design program and through apprenticeships with master seamstresses, she has a special gift for making sewing accessible and enjoyable for beginners. Her warm, patient teaching style and creative enthusiasm have inspired hundreds of students to discover the joy of creating with fabric.
