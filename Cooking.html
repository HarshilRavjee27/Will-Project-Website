Cooking

Our cooking course is designed to help learners build confidence in the kitchen, master essential techniques, and explore a variety of cuisines. Whether you are a complete beginner or looking to refine your skills, this course provides a step-by-step journey into the world of cooking.

What You'll Learn

  • Kitchen Safety & Hygiene
  • Knife Skills & Food Preparation
  • Stocks, Sauces & Soups
  • Cooking Methods (boiling, grilling, roasting, frying, steaming)
  • Baking Basics (bread, cakes, pastries)
  • International Cuisine (Italian, Indian, Asian fusion, etc.)
  • Meal Planning & Nutrition Tips
