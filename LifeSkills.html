Life Skills

This course is designed to build critical life skills that improve confidence, communication, and decision-making. You will learn about financial literacy, time management, workplace etiquette, and problem-solving. The course also emphasizes emotional intelligence, resilience, and interpersonal skills, equipping learners to thrive in both personal and professional environments.

What You'll Learn:

  • Effective communication skills
  • Financial literacy and budgeting
  • Time management and organization
  • Problem-solving and decision-making
  • Workplace etiquette and professionalism
  • Emotional intelligence and resilience
