Michael Botha

An experienced landscape designer and horticulturist with over 20 years of transforming South African outdoor spaces, Michael specializes in sustainable, indigenous landscaping. Trained through the South African Landscapers Institute and with a diploma in Horticulture, he has worked on projects ranging from small residential gardens to large commercial landscapes. His practical, down-to-earth approach and deep knowledge of South African plants and climate conditions make complex landscaping concepts easy to understand and implement. His passion for water-wise gardening and indigenous flora has helped countless homeowners and aspiring professionals create beautiful, sustainable outdoor spaces.
