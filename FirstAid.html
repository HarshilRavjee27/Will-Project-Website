CPR Training

Learn essential first aid skills, CPR techniques, wound care, emergency response, and how to handle medical emergencies professionally. This comprehensive course covers vital life-saving knowledge applicable in various real-world scenarios, empowering you to act confidently when it matters most. Perfect for domestic workers, gardeners, and anyone seeking critical safety skills.

What You'll Learn:

  • Wounds and bleeding management
  • Burns and fractures treatment
  • CPR and rescue breathing techniques
  • Emergency scene management

His patient, encouraging teaching style and real-world experience have helped thousands across South Africa gain the confidence to act in emergencies.
