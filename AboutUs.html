ABOUT US

TELLING OUR STORY FROM THE VERY BEGINNING

BEHIND OUR SUCCESS:

Precious Radebe founded The SME after watching her parents and elderly relatives work their entire lives as domestic workers and gardeners without ever having the opportunity to upskill or pursue formal education. Determined to break this cycle in her community, she created a training school that equips domestic workers and gardeners with additional skills that make them more marketable and enable them to earn higher wages. Her initiative not only helps workers negotiate better pay but also empowers them to become entrepreneurs and start their own small businesses. What began as a personal mission to honor her family's struggle has become a transformative force, giving similarly affected community members the opportunities her parents never had.
